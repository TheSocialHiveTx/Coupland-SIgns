Our Story

Family-owned, locally-operated, and dedicated to the highest standards of sign making since 1992.

30+ Years of Quality

Coupland Signs has been family owned and operated since 1992.

We've served the greater Houston area from our current location off of Main St. in La Porte since 2004. With over 30 years of experience, we provide beautiful and durable signage that helps businesses grow.

Our reliability has secured lasting relationships with many local organizations, including the City of La Porte, the La Porte Police Department, and the vast majority of La Porte's growing industrial presence.
